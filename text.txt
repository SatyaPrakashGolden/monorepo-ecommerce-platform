 Tables:
users – User accounts

addresses – User shipping/billing addresses

orders – Order records

order_items – Products in each order

payments – Payment method, status, transaction ID

coupons – Discount codes and rules

user_roles – Roles like admin, customer, vendor

product_stock_logs – Inventory change history


------------
products – Product details (name, price, variants, etc.)

brands – Brand name, logo, country

catalogs – Product groupings (e.g., "Men's Collection")

wishlists – User wishlists (userId + products)

reviews – Product ratings & comments

carts – Temporary cart items for each user

banners – Homepage banners or promotions

product_questions – User-submitted product Q&A
